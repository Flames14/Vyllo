# Privacy Policy for YMusic

Last updated: February 23, 2026

YMusic ("we", "our", or "us") is an open-source application. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information in connection with your use of the YMusic mobile application.

## 1. Information Collection and Use

**YMusic does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data or personally identifiable information (PII).**

*   **No Accounts**: You do not need to create an account to use the app.
*   **No Tracking**: We do not use any analytics, trackers, or advertising IDs.
*   **Permissions**: The app requests only the permissions necessary for its core functionality:
    *   **Internet Access**: To stream music from YouTube.
    *   **Storage Access**: To save downloaded music files locally on your device.
    *   **Notifications**: To provide playback controls in the system tray.

## 2. Third-Party Services

YMusic streams content from YouTube. When you use the app, your device connects directly to YouTube servers. Your interactions with YouTube are governed by [Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy). YMusic does not share any of your data with Google or YouTube beyond what is technically required for the streaming request (e.g., your IP address).

## 3. Data Security

Since we do not collect any data, there is no data stored on our servers to be compromised. All app settings and downloads are stored locally on your device.

## 4. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy in the repository.

## 5. Contact Us

If you have any questions or suggestions about our Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact us at the [GitHub Issues](https://github.com/Flames14/YMusic/issues) page.
